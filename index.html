ABOUT

Ecash's vision is to bring global financial freedom and this involves giving power back to the masses. However, to do this there has to be a greater adoption of the eCash crypto currency. In a very competitive Blockchain industry this is becoming increasingly hard due to the fact that eCash is a completely decentralized currency which makes it difficult to attract investment for development and advertising. So what needs to be done? Well, the community is all we have to bring about the success of this idea and that's where eFan comes in. eFan seeks to be an ecosystem that drives the adoption of eCash by using innovative technological solutions to bring about this wider adoption. For eCash to truly serve its purpose, it has to be desired for something more than its value appreciation or the it's potential to hit an all time high. It should be used to purchase products and services. With some blockchain projects, their currency has the ability to purchase NFT's and this increases the desirability of them, however with eCash until the Introduction of eFan eCash was mainly a speculative investment. The introduction of services like eFan Jobs. e2Local and more will bring greater usage of eCash and these are services eFan has set out to create.
